Email Marketing SaaS Platform

For a past year, I have increased product adoption and improved usability by designing and implementing a brand new custom design system for Insider's email marketing product

Overview

InOne Email is an email marketing product to design and launch personalized and high-performing marketing emails for eCommerce clients. Some of the world's biggest names, like BakerRoss, Marks and Spencers, etc., use InOne Email for their email marketing initiatives.

Timeline

May 2020 till April 2021

Product

for Insider

Role

• Responsible for transitioning the entire email marketing product to a new, improved UI using a brand new in-house design system.
• Constantly researched and tested the product to improve usability, reduce user pain points and improve the overall UX by designing new features or improving the existing ones.
• Design and prototype new features and parts of the product, and work closely with developers and QAs to help them implement the designs and launch them as per the product roadmap.

Research

Conducted regular user research, and focus group workshops with users, salespeople and other stakeholders of the product to uncover user needs and understand how to improve our products. There were several research methods we used in order to learn more about user's problems.

We used an intensive research documentation process to keep our insights and findings organised and accessible cross team and for product management. We used Dovetail for documentation and Google Forms or Maze for unmoderrated research. The best thing which worked for us was to get on call with the users or the stakeholders to know more about their problems first hand.

Design

Helped the email product transitioned to new design system to add consistency and streamline design-development process.

Designed new analytics dashboards that helps user measure their email campaign performance and to support data analysis of their marketing initiatives.

Shipped new features and improved minor usability issues constantly, by rigorous research and testing.

Testing

Regularly tested and conducted usability test sessions with the users, salespeople and customers of the product, to validate the designs I made.

Worked closely with QA team to participate in design UATs to squash UX development issues as well

Followed industry standard SUS and UXUM-L score methodology to measure success of the designs.

Impact

Increased product usage of email product by 12% over an year.
Improved UXUM-L score by 43% after the design system transition.
Increased efficiency of the account managers and reduced time to create campaigns by 24%
